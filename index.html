The Challenge: A Campus Divided

We have the talent. We have the drive. But we're shouting into different voids.

Siloed Communication

Dozens of WhatsApp groups, endless email chains, and scattered social media posts. Important information gets lost.

Missed Connections

A coder needs a designer. A writer needs a developer. They never meet, and incredible projects never happen.

Fragmented Resources

Event schedules, club activities, and learning materials are spread thin across the digital landscape.

The Solution: One Hub, Infinite Possibilities

A centralized platform, powered by Google's ecosystem, designed for students, by students.

AI-Powered Project Finder

Use a Gemini-powered chatbot to find project collaborators based on skills, interests, and course schedules. Your next great idea is one conversation away.

Unified Events Calendar

One integrated Google Calendar for all campus happenings. See club meetings, workshops, and official events in a single, filterable view.

Peer-to-Peer Learning Cloud

Access a library of student-created tutorials and resources, all hosted on Google Cloud. Learn from your peers and share your own expertise.

Not Just Talking, We're Building

This isn't just a concept. It's a collaborative project. We learn by doing.

Step 1: Firebase Workshop

We'll host a workshop on Firebase, and our project will be to build the real-time project board for the Hub. You'll learn and contribute simultaneously.

Step 2: Google AI Challenge

In our Google AI sessions, we won't just learn theory. We'll actively code and improve the campus chatbot that connects collaborators.

Step 3: Cloud & Community

Learn Google Cloud by building the infrastructure for our peer-to-peer tutorial library. Every workshop makes our community stronger and smarter.

This is more than an ambassador role. It's a mission to build a smarter, more connected campus. Let's make it happen together.
